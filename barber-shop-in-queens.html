Frequently Asked Questions About Barber Shop

Do you take walk-ins or should I book an appointment?
We accept both walk-ins and appointments at B & H Barber Shop. While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment, especially during peak hours (weekday evenings and weekends) to minimize wait times. You can book appointments by calling us directly at our East Village location or through our online booking system. Walk-in wait times typically range from 15-45 minutes depending on the time of day.
What are your prices for a basic men's haircut?
Our basic men's haircut starts at $30, which includes a traditional cut, neck shave, and hot towel service. Beard trims start at $15, and a full service cut and beard trim combination is $40. For first-time clients, we recommend allowing extra time for a consultation to discuss your preferred style. Senior citizens (65+) receive a $5 discount on all services Tuesday through Thursday.
How long does a typical haircut appointment take?
A standard men's haircut at B & H typically takes 30-35 minutes. This includes the consultation, shampoo (if requested), cut, styling, and final detailing. For more complex styles or additional services like beard trimming or hot shaves, please allow 45-60 minutes. We pride ourselves on being thorough while maintaining efficiency to respect our clients' time.
Do you offer hot towel straight razor shaves?
Yes, we offer traditional hot towel straight razor shaves for $35. The service takes approximately 45 minutes and includes pre-shave oil treatment, hot towel preparation, two passes with a straight razor, and a soothing aftershave treatment. All our barbers are licensed and specifically trained in straight razor shaving techniques. We use premium shaving products and fresh blades for every service.
What's your policy on children's haircuts?
We welcome children at B & H Barber Shop. Children's cuts (ages 12 and under) are priced at $25. We recommend booking appointments for children during our less busy hours (weekday mornings or early afternoons) for a more relaxed experience. Our barbers are experienced in working with kids and have special techniques to make the experience comfortable and fun. Parents are welcome to stay with their children during the cut.
