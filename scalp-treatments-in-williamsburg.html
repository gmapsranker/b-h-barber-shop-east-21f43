Frequently Asked Questions About Scalp Treatments

How much do scalp treatments cost at B & H and what's included?
Our signature scalp treatments range from $45-65 depending on the specific treatment needed. The basic treatment ($45) includes a thorough scalp analysis, deep cleansing, exfoliation, and a 10-minute scalp massage. Our premium treatment ($65) adds a specialized medicated solution for conditions like dandruff or dry scalp, plus an extended 20-minute massage and hot towel service. All treatments include a consultation to determine the best approach for your specific scalp concerns.
How long does a scalp treatment appointment typically take?
Plan for 45-60 minutes total. The initial consultation takes about 5-10 minutes, where we assess your scalp condition and discuss your concerns. The treatment itself runs 30-45 minutes depending on which service you choose. We recommend arriving 5 minutes early to your appointment, especially if it's your first visit to our Williamsburg location.
Do I need to prepare my hair/scalp in any special way before coming in?
For best results, please come with clean, product-free hair. If possible, avoid using heavy styling products for 24 hours before your treatment. However, if you're dealing with specific issues like severe dandruff or scalp buildup, leave your scalp in its natural state so we can properly assess the condition. Don't worry about washing your hair right before - we'll handle that as part of the treatment.
How often should I get a scalp treatment to see results?
For most clients, we recommend starting with treatments every 2-3 weeks for the first two months to address immediate concerns. After that, monthly maintenance treatments are typically sufficient. However, this can vary based on your specific scalp condition - some clients with severe dandruff or dry scalp might need weekly treatments initially. During your first visit, we'll create a personalized treatment plan based on your scalp's needs.
Are your scalp treatments good for hair loss or thinning hair?
While our scalp treatments can help create optimal conditions for healthy hair growth by improving scalp health, they're not a cure for genetic hair loss. Our treatments are effective at addressing issues like scalp tension, buildup, and poor circulation - factors that can contribute to thinning hair. We use professional-grade products and techniques that can help stimulate follicles and improve scalp health. For serious hair loss concerns, we can also recommend trusted dermatologists in the Williamsburg area who specialize in hair loss treatment.
