Frequently Asked Questions About Beard Trimming & Shaping

How much does a beard trim and shape typically cost at B & H?
Our beard trim and shape service is $30. This includes a full consultation, precision trimming, line-up work, and final styling. For first-time clients, we recommend allowing extra time for a detailed consultation to understand your preferred beard style and maintenance routine. We also offer a combined haircut and beard service for $55.
Do I need an appointment for a beard trim, or do you take walk-ins?
While we do accept walk-ins when possible, we strongly recommend booking an appointment, especially during peak hours (weekdays 4-7pm and weekends). You can book online through our website or call us at our East Village location. We typically reserve a few spots daily for walk-ins, but wait times can be 30-45 minutes during busy periods.
How long does a typical beard trim and shape service take?
A standard beard trim and shape service takes approximately 25-30 minutes. This includes our consultation time to discuss your desired style, the actual trimming and shaping process, and final detailing. If you're coming in for the first time or want a significant style change, please allow 35-40 minutes for a more thorough consultation.
What should I do to prepare my beard before coming in for a trim?
For best results, please come in with your beard clean and dry. We recommend washing it the night before or morning of your appointment. Don't apply any heavy oils or balms before your visit, as this can affect how the hair lays and cuts. If possible, bring a photo of your desired beard style. Also, try not to trim your beard yourself for at least 2 weeks before your appointment to give us enough length to work with.
Do you offer any guarantees on your beard trimming services?
Yes, we stand behind our work. If you're not completely satisfied with your beard trim, please let us know immediately or within 48 hours of your service, and we'll gladly make adjustments at no additional cost. Our barbers are licensed professionals with specific training in beard care and styling. However, please note that significant changes in length cannot be reversed, so we always err on the conservative side during initial trims.
