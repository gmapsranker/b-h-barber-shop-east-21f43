Frequently Asked Questions About Wedding & Event Grooming Packages

What are your wedding grooming package prices and what's included?
Our wedding grooming packages start at $75 per person for the groom and $65 for groomsmen. The Classic Wedding Package includes a precision haircut, hot towel straight razor shave, styling, and a complimentary beer or whiskey. Our Premium Package ($95) adds a facial massage and deep conditioning treatment. For wedding parties of 4 or more, we offer a 10% group discount. We recommend booking wedding services 2-3 weeks in advance to secure your preferred time slot.
Do you travel to wedding venues in the Astoria/Queens area for on-site grooming?
Yes, we offer mobile grooming services within a 10-mile radius of our East Village location for wedding parties of 4 or more. Our mobile service fee starts at $150 plus individual grooming costs. We bring all necessary equipment and products. For venues in Manhattan, there's an additional $50 travel fee. We require a 50% deposit to secure mobile bookings and need access to proper lighting and electrical outlets.
How far in advance should I schedule a trial run for wedding grooming?
We recommend scheduling your trial run 6-8 weeks before your wedding date. This gives us time to perfect your look and make any adjustments needed. The trial includes a full consultation about your desired style, a test run of the haircut and grooming services, and photos for reference. Trial runs are $45 and can be credited toward your wedding package if booked within 2 months.
What time do you open, and can you accommodate early morning wedding appointments?
Our regular hours are Tuesday-Saturday 9am-7pm, and Sunday 10am-5pm (closed Mondays). For wedding parties, we offer special early morning appointments starting at 7am by request. There's a $50 early opening fee for appointments before 9am. We're located at 28-32 31st Street in Astoria, with street parking available and just two blocks from the N/W Broadway station.
What happens if I need to reschedule my wedding grooming appointment?
We understand plans can change. For wedding services, we require 72 hours notice for rescheduling without penalty. Changes made with less than 72 hours notice will incur a 25% rebooking fee. In case of emergency situations, we'll work with you to accommodate changes when possible. All wedding bookings require a 25% deposit to secure the date, which is fully refundable with 1 week's notice of cancellation.
