Frequently Asked Questions About Hot Towel Shaves

How much does a hot towel shave cost and what's included?
Our signature hot towel shave is $35 and takes approximately 30-35 minutes. The service includes pre-shave oil application, two passes with a straight razor, multiple hot towel applications, aftershave balm, and a soothing facial massage. We use premium shaving products from Proraso and Art of Shaving to ensure the most comfortable experience possible.
Do I need to make an appointment for a hot towel shave?
While we accept walk-ins when possible, we strongly recommend booking appointments for hot towel shaves, especially during peak hours (weekdays after 5pm and weekends). You can book online through our website or call us at (718) 555-0123. We're located in Queens at 123 Steinway Street and are open Tuesday-Saturday 9am-7pm.
How often should I get a professional hot towel shave?
For the best results, we recommend getting a professional hot towel shave every 2-3 weeks, depending on your hair growth rate. However, many of our clients come in for special occasions or monthly maintenance. If you have sensitive skin or any skin conditions, we can adjust our technique and products accordingly - just let your barber know.
Are your barbers licensed and experienced in straight razor shaving?
Yes, all our barbers are licensed by the New York State Division of Licensing Services and have undergone specific training in straight razor shaving. Our lead barbers have 10+ years of experience in traditional hot towel shaves. We also maintain strict sanitation protocols and use fresh, sterilized razors for each client.
What if I experience irritation after my shave?
While rare, if you experience any irritation after your shave, please contact us immediately. We offer a 48-hour satisfaction guarantee and will provide a complimentary touch-up if needed. We keep detailed notes on each client's skin sensitivity and preferred products to ensure the best experience possible. For first-time clients, we recommend doing a small patch test if you have sensitive skin.
