Frequently Asked Questions About Barber Shop

Do you take walk-ins or should I make an appointment?
We accept both walk-ins and appointments. However, we strongly recommend booking an appointment, especially during peak hours (weekday evenings and weekends) to avoid waiting. You can book online through our website or call us at (718) XXX-XXXX. Walk-ins are served on a first-come, first-served basis, and wait times can range from 15-45 minutes during busy periods.
What are your prices for a basic men's haircut?
Our standard men's haircut is $35, which includes a hot towel finish and neck cleanup. Beard trims are $20, and a full service (haircut + beard trim) is $50. We also offer senior citizen discounts at $28 for a basic cut. All services include a consultation to ensure we understand exactly what style you're looking for.
How long does a typical haircut appointment take?
A standard men's haircut typically takes 30-35 minutes. This includes the consultation, shampoo (if requested), cut, and styling. For more complex styles or additional services like beard trimming, please allow 45-60 minutes. We take pride in not rushing our cuts while still maintaining efficiency.
Do you have experience with cutting textured/ethnic hair?
Yes, absolutely! Our barbers are professionally trained and experienced in cutting all hair types and textures. We have extensive experience with African American hair, curly hair, and all ethnic hair types. Several of our barbers specialize in fade techniques and textured hair styling. We recommend asking for Tony or Marcus, who have over 15 years of experience with textured hair cuts.
What forms of payment do you accept, and is there parking nearby?
We accept all major credit cards, cash, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For parking, there's metered street parking on East 7th Street, and there's also a parking garage two blocks away on 2nd Avenue. We're also easily accessible by subway - just two blocks from the L train at 1st Avenue station.
