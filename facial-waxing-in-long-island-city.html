Frequently Asked Questions About Facial Waxing

How much does facial waxing cost at B & H Barber Shop?
Our facial waxing services start at $15 for eyebrows, $12 for upper lip, and $25 for a full-face wax (excluding eyebrows). We offer a combo package for eyebrows and upper lip at $25. Senior citizens receive a 10% discount on all waxing services. Prices may vary slightly depending on hair thickness and area coverage needed.
Do I need an appointment for facial waxing, or can I walk in?
While we accept walk-ins for our barber services, we recommend booking appointments for facial waxing to ensure minimal wait time. You can schedule by calling us at our East Village location or booking online through our website. We typically reserve early morning and late afternoon slots for waxing services. Walk-ins are accepted if there's availability, but wait times may vary during peak hours (weekends and after 5 PM on weekdays).
How long does the facial waxing service take?
Eyebrow waxing typically takes 10-15 minutes, while a full-face wax service takes approximately 25-30 minutes. We recommend arriving 5 minutes early to discuss any specific concerns or desired shapes with your waxing specialist. First-time clients should allow an extra 5-10 minutes for a brief consultation about skin sensitivity and aftercare.
What type of wax do you use and is it safe for sensitive skin?
We use premium hard wax specifically designed for facial hair removal. This type of wax adheres to the hair rather than the skin, making it gentler and ideal for sensitive areas. Our waxing specialists are licensed by New York State and trained in proper sanitation procedures. We always perform a small patch test for first-time clients to ensure no adverse reactions. If you have particularly sensitive skin or are using any topical medications, please let us know before your service.
How long will the results last after facial waxing?
Most clients enjoy smooth results for 3-4 weeks after facial waxing. However, this can vary depending on individual hair growth patterns and thickness. We recommend scheduling regular appointments every 4-6 weeks to maintain results and achieve finer hair regrowth over time. For best results, avoid direct sun exposure and heavy skincare products for 24 hours after waxing. We provide complimentary aftercare instructions and product recommendations specific to your skin type.
