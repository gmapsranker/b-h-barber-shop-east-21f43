Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Coloring & Highlights

How much do hair coloring services typically cost at B & H Barber Shop?
Our men's hair coloring services start at $45 for basic gray coverage, $65 for full color, and $85+ for highlights. Exact pricing depends on hair length, desired service, and amount of product needed. We offer free consultations to provide an accurate quote based on your specific needs and hair type. All prices include a finish cut and style.
How long does a hair coloring appointment usually take?
Most color services at B & H take between 60-90 minutes total. Basic gray coverage typically takes about an hour, while full color or highlights can take up to 90 minutes. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekend appointments. Our East Village location is busiest on Saturdays, so weekday appointments often have more flexibility.
Do I need to do anything special to prepare for my hair coloring appointment?
For best results, come with unwashed hair (24-48 hours since last wash is ideal). Wear a shirt you don't mind getting color on, though we do provide protective capes. If you're getting highlights or a dramatic color change, bringing reference photos is helpful. We recommend scheduling a quick consultation before major color changes so we can assess your hair and discuss expectations.
Are your colorists licensed and experienced with men's hair coloring?
Yes, all our colorists at B & H are NJ state-licensed and specifically trained in men's hair coloring techniques. Our senior colorists each have 5+ years of experience with men's color services. We regularly attend continuing education to stay current with the latest techniques and products. We specialize in natural-looking results that work well for professional environments.
What if I'm not happy with my hair color results?
We offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee on all our color services. If you're not completely satisfied with your results, return within 7 days and we'll adjust the color at no additional charge. We take detailed notes on your color formula and preferences to ensure consistency with future appointments. Please note that major color corrections may require additional fees, which we'll discuss during your consultation.
